# Privacy Policy for RainIQ

**Effective Date:** 2026-05-04

Welcome to RainIQ. Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our mobile application.

## 1. Information We Collect
- **Personal Information:** We may collect your name, email address, and location data if you choose to provide it for community features or personalized insights.
- **Usage Data:** We automatically collect data on how you interact with the app, including water usage logs, settings preferences, and interaction with various features.
- **Device Information:** We may collect information about your mobile device, including the hardware model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers to improve app performance and troubleshoot issues.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
We use the collected information to:
- Provide, maintain, and improve the functionality of RainIQ.
- Personalize your experience by offering tailored water conservation tips and insights.
- Analyze usage trends and patterns to enhance our features.
- Communicate with you regarding updates, security alerts, and support messages.

## 3. Data Storage and Security
We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Your data is securely stored, and we do not sell your personal information to third parties.

## 4. Third-Party Services
We may use third-party services (such as analytics providers or cloud hosting) that have access to your information solely to perform tasks on our behalf. These third parties are bound by strict confidentiality agreements.

## 5. Your Choices and Rights
You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information. You can manage your data preferences within the app settings. You may also opt out of certain data collection processes, though this may limit your ability to use specific features.

## 6. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy in the app or via other communication channels.

## 7. Contact Us
If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at support@jal-sanchay.app.
